Bio

I am a visual artist based in Kankaanpää, Finland, currently studying fine arts at Satakunnan University of Applied Sciences. Through grotesque yet empathetic characters, my work explores the inner movements of the mind—thoughts, emotions, and psychological states that blur the line between reality and the subconscious.

Working primarily with oil and acrylic, I blend sensitivity, irony, and raw physicality. My practice spans over eleven years, including formal training at Helsinki's Art School, where I completed the comprehensive art education program and exhibited my graduation work in 2021.
